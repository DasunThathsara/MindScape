Fish pose (Matsyasana)

Instructions

  1. Lie flat on your back with your legs straight and arms resting by your sides.
  2. Bend your knees and place your feet flat on the floor, bringing them as close to your buttocks as possible.
  3. Lift your hips slightly off the ground and slide your hands palms-down underneath your buttocks.
  4. Press your forearms and elbows firmly into the ground, and inhale as you lift your chest towards the ceiling.
  5. Tilt your head back and rest the crown of your head on the ground, or lift your head off the ground and gently arch your neck.
  6. Hold the pose for 5-10 deep breaths, then slowly release by lowering your chest and head back to the ground.
