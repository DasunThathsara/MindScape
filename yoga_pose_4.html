Low Lunge (Anjaneyasana)

Instructions

  1. Begin in a high plank position, with your hands directly under your shoulders and your feet hip-width apart.
  2. Step your right foot forward between your hands, making sure your knee is directly above your ankle.
  3. Lower your left knee to the ground, keeping your toes curled under.
  4. Inhale and lift your torso upright, bringing your arms up overhead with your palms facing each other.
  5. Sink your hips down towards the ground, feeling a stretch in the front of your left hip and thigh.
  6. Hold the pose for 5-10 breaths, then release by placing hands on the ground and stepping back into high plank.
  7. Repeat the pose on the other side by stepping your left foot forward and lowering your right knee to the ground.
