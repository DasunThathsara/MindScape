Happy Duet

Goal is to find pairs of identical objects. The player is presented with a grid of facedown cards, and they must turn them over two at a time, trying to find a match.

Flappy Bird

Players control a bird by tapping the screen to flap its wings and avoid obstacles. The goal is to achieve the highest score possible. It's known for its difficulty and addictive gameplay.

Tetris

As lines are completed, they disappear, and the player earns points. The game ends when the stack of blocks reaches the top of the screen.
